University of the Cordilleras - Philippine Air Force Reserve Officer Training Corps Unit

Mission

To develop capable, disciplined, and service-oriented cadets who are prepared to support the Armed Forces of the Philippines and contribute to national development.

Vision

To be a premier ROTC unit that molds future leaders grounded in patriotism, integrity, and excellence.

What is ROTC?

The Reserve Officer Training Corps (ROTC) is a program designed to provide military training to tertiary level students in order to motivate, train, organize, and mobilize them for national defense preparedness. It is one of the three components of the National Service Training Program (NSTP), authorized under Republic Act No. 9163.

Legal Basis

  • Philippine National Defense Act of 1935
  • Republic Act 7077 (Citizen Armed Forces of the Philippines Reservist Act)
  • Republic Act 9163 (NSTP Act of 2001)

Exemptions from ROTC

  • Non-Filipino citizens or nationals
  • Barangay / Sangguniang Kabataan officials (with required documentation)
